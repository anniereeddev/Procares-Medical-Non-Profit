Attend the Benefit

We are excited to share an innovative educational initiative aimed at guiding medical professionals in diagnosing these illnesses. We are so grateful for the support of a prominent figure in the medical field, Dr. Craig Colby. His efforts have aided numerous individuals in similar situations, ensuring accurate diagnoses, and appropriate treatments. Join us in spreading awareness by reposting this vital information. Educating healthcare providers on identification and management can alleviate the suffering of thousands. While a cure may be elusive for Ally, her quality of life has significantly improved.

This event is Dr. Craig Colby's vision for helping medical providers diagnose all of those that are in need of help with Ehlers-Danlos syndrome (EDS), POTS, Vascular Compressions and MCAS. Whether attending in person, virtually, providing financial assistance, or facilitating connections for healthcare providers, every effort counts. Together, let's impact lives one at a time.
