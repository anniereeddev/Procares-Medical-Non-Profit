Meet Ally

Ally has Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome, Hypermobile type, and suffers from related conditions such as Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome (POTS), chronic pain, spontaneous intracranial hypotension (SIH), and several abdominal compression syndromes. During her childhood, many pediatric specialists and providers dismissed her symptoms as being psychosomatic. In this video, Ally shares her journey and emphasizes the crucial role knowledgeable physicians played in her healing process.

Cole's Story

True Story: Cole has POTS (Postural Tachycardia Syndrome) and struggles to find help, becoming disabled by it. Pediatric providers kept telling him it was all in his head. But once he found knowledgeable care providers who knew what to do, he found healing.

Attend the Benefit

We are excited to share an innovative educational initiative aimed at guiding medical professionals in diagnosing these illnesses. We are so grateful for the support of a prominent figure in the medical field, Dr. Craig Colby. His efforts have aided numerous individuals in similar situations, ensuring accurate diagnoses, and appropriate treatments.

Join us in spreading awareness by reposting this vital information. Educating healthcare providers on identification and management can alleviate the suffering of thousands. While a cure may be elusive for Ally, her quality of life has significantly improved.

This event is Dr. Craig Colby's vision for helping medical providers diagnose all of those that are in need of help with Ehlers-Danlos syndrome (EDS), POTS, Vascular Compressions and MCAS. Whether attending in person, virtually, providing financial assistance, or facilitating connections for healthcare providers, every effort counts. Together, let's impact lives one at a time.
