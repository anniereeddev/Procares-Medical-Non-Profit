Dr. Warner is a triple board-certified physician specializing in general and cardiotheracic anesthesiology and interventional pain medicine. He graduated from the Univeristy of Utah having studied in both the school of business and college of science. He went on to graduate with honors from Ross University School of Medicine, followed by anesthesiology residency at the University of Texas. He completed additional fellowship training in cardiothoracic anesthesia and interventional pain medicine at Yale University and MD Anderson Cancer Center, respectively.

Lauren Miller

Lauren loves her family, frenchies, and finance. She has a BS in honors finance from the University of Utah. She worked in private equity for a few years before going back to school to earn on MBA from George Washington University. Since graduating from GWU in 2021, she has worked for the Larry H and Gail Mill Family Foundation doing private asset allocation. She has a sister with dysautonomia diseases and she has watched her suffer for the past decade. It has been a family fight and Lauren is passionate in doing what she can to help further the research and doctor education surrounding this disease state. Lauren has a wonderful husband, Josh, two young kids, and two French bulldogs. It's a fun and chaotic household.
